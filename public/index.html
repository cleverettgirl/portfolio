I’m a detail-oriented software engineer from Florida living in New York City.


I completed my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Sustainability at the University of Florida. After I finished, I started playing around with building simple websites.


I started to really enjoy the process of creating websites and soon spent hours admiring subtle animations and the small details on a site I would find. This propelled me to learn more, especially Javascript, which led me to Grace Hopper.


Out of this new passion, I discovered how much I love creating— everything from making the internal mechanics work to implementing stunning and simple designs.


My ultimate goal is to work with a group of incredibly talented people, soak up their awesomeness and collectively make the world a better place.


I’m an avid supporter of equal rights, promoting diversity in tech, childhood education and sustainability.


When I’m not in front of a computer screen, I’m probably painting, eating good food that I didn’t make, volunteering, traveling or playing with all the puppies and kittens.



Skills


I love learning new ways to make my code cleaner and faster. I specialize in full stack Javascript, specifically Node.js, Express.js, React.js, Redux and Sequelize.

Riddikulus




Riddikulus is a Harry Potter inspired prototypal e-commerce site that sells magical beasts. This was a collaborative effort between me and two classmates. I implemented orders and orderlines models, local and 3rd party sign up/log in functionalities and incorporated multiple components for our front-end. This site was made primarily with Node.js, Express, React, Redux, Sequelize and Bootstrap.

Snakes With Friends




Snakes with Friends is a real-time, in browser game that allows multiple players to connect with their cell phones to a single, shared screen and battle to the death. This was a collaborative effort between me and three classmates and was built mainly with Node.js, Socket.io, P5, React and Redux.
